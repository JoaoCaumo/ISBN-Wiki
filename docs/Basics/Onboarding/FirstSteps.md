# Welcome
We are thrilled to have you onboard! In this wiki, we'll cover the information necessary to help you understand the team, communication channels and the tools we use for work.

## Getting Started
When you enter our team, you will be assigned to a buddy to help you out. Any doubts or assistant you may need, the buddy should be your go to. But don't be shy, you can ask anyone in the team for help!

## Our Mission
ISBN Product Security safeguards SAP Intelligent Spend and Business Network (ISBN) products by embedding security throughout the software development lifecycle and championing secure-by-design principles. We partner with engineering and product teams to conduct threat modeling, architecture reviews, security testing, and vulnerability remediation. Our mission is to reduce risk and uphold compliance and customer trust.

## Products We Support
**SAP Ariba solutions:** Buying and Invoicing, Sourcing, Contracts, Supplier Lifecycle and Performance, Supplier Risk, Spend Analysis, Supply Chain Collaboration, Commerce Automation  
**SAP Fieldglass:** Contingent Workforce Management, Services Procurement, Worker Profile Management  
**SAP Concur:** Expense, Travel, Invoice  
**SAP Business Network:** for Procurement, for Logistics, for Asset

## Tools
Our core stack centers on **Jira** for task visibility and control, with **Splunk** and **Tenable** supporting our vulnerability workflow. This setup gives us a single source of truth for work items and findings.

## Communication
The team's main communication happens via **Microsoft Teams**; we have three meeting with the whole team per week, to share findings and updates.
